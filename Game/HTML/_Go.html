What is Go?

Go is an ancient Chinese board game that is revered in some Asian cultures, as chess is in western cultures. The parameters of play are some of the simplest, yet the methods in which you play these moves can be more complex than proving P=NP[[]]. It is a game that will suck you in from the beginning, but prove daunting if you dare continue. A game played on a grid of lines that make up 19x19 points, played by its two players consecutively placing their ‘stones’ wherever they wish, hoping to gain more territory than the other. It is known as Go, Baduk, and Weiqi; and has only recently been dominated by AI[[]]. It is a beautiful game for simple people.
